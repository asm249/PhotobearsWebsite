Optics Research at Berkeley

Berkeley faculty who are actively involved in optics research are from the departments of Astronomy, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Vision Science and Optometry. Selected optical faculty members along with their personal and/or lab websites are listed below. Perspective graduate students who are interested in studying optics at UC Berkeley are suggested to first identify a faculty member whose research program is of interest and the department to which he/she belongs to. Each department has its own application process so please visit each affiliation's website for more information on how to apply.
