Welcome to Photobears!

We are the joint student chapter of the SPIE (International Society for Optical Engineering) and OSA (Optical Society of America) at the University of California, Berkeley. The club is student-operated by individuals who are dedicated to advancing the field of optics. Our members are affiliated with several departments on campus including Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Vision Science and Optometry. We are devoted to helping the optics community on campus.
